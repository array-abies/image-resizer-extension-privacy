Privacy Policy for Image Resizer Extension

Effective Date: March 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the "Image Resizer" Google Chrome/Edge Extension (hereinafter referred to as "the Extension") developed by Array.

1. Data Collection and Usage

The Extension is designed with strict privacy principles. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data, images, or clipboard content to any external servers.

The Extension requests the following permissions strictly for its core functionality:

  • Clipboard Read/Write: Used exclusively to access the image you have copied to your clipboard, resize it, and write the compressed image back to your clipboard so you can paste it.
  • Storage: Used to save your personal preferences (e.g., target width/height, specified domains) locally within your own browser.

2. Local Processing Only

All image resizing and processing algorithms run 100% locally on your device within the browser's environment. Your images never leave your computer.

3. Third-Party Services

The Extension does not integrate with any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.
